Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our classic car hire services.

Absolutely! We encourage all clients to visit our Surrey showroom to view and experience our vehicles firsthand. We offer viewing appointments Monday through Saturday, and our team will be happy to show you the full collection and discuss your specific requirements. Please call us at least 24 hours in advance to schedule your visit.

Professional chauffeur service is available for all our vehicles and is included in our wedding packages. Our chauffeurs are fully licensed, uniformed, and experienced with classic vehicles. For self-drive options, we require a clean driving license and minimum age of 25. All drivers must be approved and briefed on the specific vehicle before collection.

We primarily serve Surrey, London, and the surrounding Home Counties including Hampshire, Berkshire, and Sussex. For events beyond our standard service area, additional travel charges may apply. We're happy to discuss specific locations and provide detailed quotes for destination weddings or events further afield.

All our vehicles are covered by comprehensive commercial insurance with £5 million public liability coverage. This includes third-party liability, vehicle damage, and passenger protection. For self-drive rentals, we require proof of valid insurance or can arrange additional coverage. Full details of our insurance policies are provided with every booking confirmation.

For weddings, we recommend booking 6-12 months in advance, especially for peak season (May through September). Corporate events and film productions can often be accommodated with shorter notice, subject to availability. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible, so please don't hesitate to contact us even if your event is approaching quickly.

All our vehicles undergo rigorous pre-event inspections and maintenance. In the unlikely event of a mechanical issue, we have backup vehicles available and 24/7 roadside assistance. For critical events like weddings, we can arrange a backup vehicle to be on standby. Our commitment is to ensure your special day proceeds without interruption.
